Dinosaur Game Clone (Guide)

  • Press any key to start the game.
  • Press the up arrow or space bar to jump over the obstacles (cactus) in your path.
  • The longer you play, the faster dino runs/the ground moves.
  • Once you crashes into the cactus, the game is over and you have to restart (your score resets, too). You can restart the game by pressing any key.
